Welcome to my site!

Hi, this is Zhuohuang Zhang (Chinese: 张焯煌, pronounced as /ʈʂɑŋˉ·dʒɔˉ·xwɑŋˊ/) from Changsha, China. I am a fifth-year (final-year) PhD student in Department of Speech, Language and Hearing Sciences and Department of Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington. My supervisors are Prof. Yi Shen from Department of Speech, Language and Hearing Sciences (now at UW Seattle), and Prof. Donald Williamson from Department of Computer Science.

Research Interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests include speech/audio processing, speech enhancement/separation (e.g., speech denoising and dereverberation), deep learning and human speech perception under adverse acoustic conditions.
