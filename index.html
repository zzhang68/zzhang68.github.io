Welcome to my site!

Hi, I am currently a fourth-year PhD student in department of Speech and Hearing Sciences and department of Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington. My supervisors are Dr. Yi Shen from Department of Speech, Language and Hearing Sciences (now at UW), and Dr. Donald Williamson from Department of Computer Science.

Research Interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests include speech/audio processing, speech enhancement/separation (e.g., speech denoising and dereverberation), deep learning and human speech perception under adverse acoustic conditions.
